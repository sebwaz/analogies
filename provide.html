I’d build you a house
but there isn’t much these arms can lift

I’d buy you a datsun
but there isn’t much this wallet fits

I’ll fly you away from here
just let me save to buy the tickets

I’ll lie with you when
my body is all I have to give

you have my shoulder and my ear
I’ll gather pretty words for you to hear
there’s nothing else I can provide for you
